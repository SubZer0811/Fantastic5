Yummy Foods



Our website is all about food ordering from our institute's Cafeteria.

In our database, we have 3 entities, and 2 relationships. They are as follows:



Entities:

1. Customer

Its attributes are as follows:

a. Hostel (Ashwatha/Ashoka/Jasmine)

b. c_id (Phone number of Customer - Primary Key)

c. Name (For identification)


2. Menu

Its attributes are as follows:

a. Price (Cost)

b. fid (ID given to each item - Primary Key)

3. Item (Food)


3. Stock

Its attributes are as follows:

a. fid (Foreign Key)

b. Quantity (Quantity of food available)




Relationships


1. Order

Its attributes are as follows:

a. order_id (Unique Order number generated)

b. c_id (Foreign Key)

c. f_id (Foreign Key)

d. quantity (Amount of items ordered by Customer)


2. Availability

Checks if the required quantity needed by customer is available
